Hip Dysplasia Orthopedic Condition

Canine Hip Dysplasia (CHD)

Hip dysplasia is a genetic skeletal condition where the hip joint fails to develop properly, leading to joint instability and eventual arthritis. It's one of the most common orthopedic conditions in dogs, particularly affecting large and giant breeds.
